Our Story

Liam and Nicole met while both out with their groups of friends at a bar in Phoenix.
After catching each other’s eye across the room and spending the rest of the night finding out how much they have in common, they started dating.
Two years later, we're best friends, true loves, and badass partners in life. We're finally ready to make it official!

And the rest is history!

Her Story


Nicole Pillai (soon-to-be Williams) grew up in the Bay Area, spending much of her childhood in Palo Alto and Cupertino.
She studied at and worked in New York for two years before going to get her MBA at in Fontainebleau and Singapore.

His Story


Liam Williams was born in Mumbai and spent much of his life in Hong Kong, moving to Paris at the age of 18 to study fine art.
His work is known for blurring the line between technology and art and has been selected
for display at exhibitions in Paris, Shanghai, and New York.
